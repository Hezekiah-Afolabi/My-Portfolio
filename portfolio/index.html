Industrial Safety Compliance Framework

Risk Assessment Tools — Safety Management Systems — Compliance Software — Data Analysis

Groundwater Protection & Watershed Vulnerability Assessment

Comprehensive groundwater protection research in semi-arid systems, characterizing hydrological and agronomic controls influencing solute transport. Established multi-site volumetric water content monitoring networks and conducted hydraulic conductivity measurements. Developed and calibrated HYDRUS-1D models simulating coupled water flow and solute transport. Performed multi-criteria spatial vulnerability mapping integrating soil data, irrigation classifications, and agrochemical application records to identify critical recharge zones and nonpoint source pollution risks.

HYDRUS-1D — ArcGIS — LI-COR Eddy Covariance — SATURO Infiltrometer — Campbell Scientific — R — SSURGO — Remote Sensing
